Free 1984 Apple Macintosh Mockup (PSD)

1984 Apple Macintosh MockupReleasing the 1984 Apple Macintosh was a turning point for Apple Inc. And it’s hard to forget such a creation that changed the way we view and access electronic devices. And to recall what was known as a “watershed event” and a “masterpiece” at the time, Anthony Boyd Graphics share a new PSD mockup that features the famous Apple Macintosh.This item is remarkable but it’s also set within an aesthetic scene created by the vintage elements from the background map to the rugged notebooks set to the side. But don’t just limit yourself to a simple copy/paste process using the smart objects, but customize the screen as well with its reflections and shadows to make sure that what you’re sharing is a completely realistic presentation of your design. When it was released, the Macintosh was bundled with two main applications: MacPaint and MacWrite, so you can pay homage to the original release with uncomplicated designs that amuse all those who were familiar with the device or you can display simple but modern UI designs for a break in the flow.
